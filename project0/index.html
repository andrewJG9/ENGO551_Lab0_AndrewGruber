Life and Work of Andrew Gruber

By Andrew Gruber

Andrew Gruber is a professional college student and air breather based in Calgary, Albera. Born in 1999 in the middle of the Northwest Passage to parents Adam and Adamette Sandler, Andrew showed a talent for drinking water and coloring outside the lines. Due to his habit for bawling whenever near a civic. Andrew was extremely popular amongst his peers, often called dairy boy as a term of endearment. At 6 years old, Andrew ran for congress. He was unfortunetely disqualified from contention as one must be able to recite all the lyrics to the hit song Take on Me. Following his campaign, Andrew returned to the second grade and promptly was given the key to the city for his work in advanced crayon eating. Now a local celebrity, thanks to his ability to consume both Spring Green and Sunset Orange crayons, Andrew focused his newfound fame and wealth towards research into advanced particle physics.
