Music Career

Following his career as a bike rider, Andrew made a splash in the music industry. With a wide vocal range and a talent for the Banjo, Andrew produced several #1 billboard hits. He charted in over 40 countries, including: Switzerland, Canada, Mexico and Japan. He produced the entirety of Beethoven's 9th symphony and was a ghostwriter for Bach.

List of Grammy Accolades:

  • Best Polka Album 2020
  • Best German Reggae Single 2022
  • Best Medival Folk Rock Album 2020
  • Best Disco Performance
